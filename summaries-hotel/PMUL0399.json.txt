

1. Ask the customer if they have a preferred area in Cambridge.

2. If the customer does have a preferred area, ask if they have any other restrictions (e.g. need free parking).

3. Once the customer has given you their preferences, search for available places that meet their criteria.

4. Once you have found a place, ask the customer if they would like you to book it for them.

5. If the customer would like you to book it, ask for how many people and how many nights they would like to stay.

6. Make the booking and provide the customer with the relevant information (e.g. car type, contact number).
