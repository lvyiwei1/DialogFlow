

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are:

1. Asking the customer what type of room they are looking for
2. Asking the customer what price range they are looking for
3. Asking the customer what day they will be arriving
4. Asking the customer if they need any additional services
5. Booking the hotel room for the customer
6. Giving the customer the reference number for their reservation
7.Asking the customer if they need any further assistance
