

1. Ask the customer what type of hotel they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer what their budget is.

3. Ask the customer if they have any specific location requirements.

4. Ask the customer if they have any specific amenity requirements.

5. If the customer has no specific requirements, recommend a hotel that meets their budget.

6. If the customer has specific requirements, recommend a hotel that meets their budget and requirements.

7. Assist the customer in booking their stay.
