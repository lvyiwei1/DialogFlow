2. If the business does not have any hotels that meet the customer's specifications, ask the customer if they would like a different area or star rating.
3. If the customer would like a different star rating, recommend a hotel within the customer's price range.
4. Ask the customer what day they are arriving and if they would like to stay for more than one night.
5. Book the hotel and give the customer the reference number.
6. Ask the customer if they need any additional assistance.
