

helping a customer book a hotel

1. Ask the customer what kind of hotel they are looking for.
2. Ask the customer how many people will be staying in the hotel room.
3. Ask the customer how many nights they will be staying in the hotel room.
4. Ask the customer what day they will be checking into the hotel room.
5. Ask the customer if they have any specific requests for their hotel room.
6. Find a hotel room that meets the customer's needs.
7. Book the hotel room for the customer.
8. Give the customer the reference number for their hotel room reservation.
