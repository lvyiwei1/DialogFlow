

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are:

1. Ask the customer what type of hotel they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer what their budget is.

3. Ask the customer if they have any specific requirements (e.g. free wifi, free parking).

4. Based on the customer's answers, recommend a few hotels.

5. Ask the customer to choose a hotel.

6. Ask the customer to confirm the booking details.
