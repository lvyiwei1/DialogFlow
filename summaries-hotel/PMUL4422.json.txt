

1. Ask the customer if they need help booking a hotel.
2. If the customer does need help, ask them what their budget is for the hotel.
3. Ask the customer if they have any specific requirements for the hotel (e.g. free parking, free internet, etc.).
4. Based on the customer's budget and requirements, suggest a few hotels.
5. Ask the customer to choose one of the hotels.
6. Make a reservation for the customer.
7. Give the customer the reference number for the reservation.
8. Thank the customer for their business.
