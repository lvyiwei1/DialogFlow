

1. Greet customer and ask what they are looking for.

2. Ask customer if they have any preferences for location, type of accommodation, etc.

3. Provide customer with options that fit their preferences.

4. Ask customer if they would like to book a reservation.

5. If yes, ask for details such as number of people, length of stay, etc.

6. Make the reservation and provide the customer with a reference number.

7. Ask customer if there is anything else they need.
