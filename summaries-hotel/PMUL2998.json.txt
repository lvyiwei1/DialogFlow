

1. Ask the customer if they know the name of the hotel they are looking for.
2. Look up the hotel online.
3. Give the customer the location and price range of the hotel.
4. Ask the customer if they are interested in the hotel.
5. If the customer is not interested, ask if they have a preference on the area.
6. Find a hotel in the area and price range that the customer is looking for.
7. Ask the customer if they are interested in the hotel.
8. If the customer is not interested, ask if they would like you to look for another hotel.
9. Repeat steps 6-8 until the customer is satisfied.
10. Thank the customer for their inquiry.
