

1. Ask the customer for the name of the hotel they are looking for.
2. Find the phone number for the hotel.
3. Ask the customer if they would like you to book a reservation for them.
4. If the hotel is unavailable, ask the customer if they would like you to look for another hotel in the same price range.
5. If the customer would like you to book a different hotel, find a hotel in the same price range and book it for the customer.
6. Get the reference number for the booking from the hotel.
7. Give the reference number to the customer.
