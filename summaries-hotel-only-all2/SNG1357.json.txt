

1. Ask the customer what their budget is.

2. Ask the customer what their preferred location is.

3. Ask the customer if they have a particular hotel in mind.

4. If the customer does not have a particular hotel in mind, provide them with a list of options within their budget and preferred location.

5. Once the customer has selected a hotel, ask them how many people will be staying and for how many nights.

6. Book the room for the customer and provide them with a reference number.
