

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are as follows:

1. Find out what kind of hotel the customer is looking for.

2. Find out if the customer has any specific requirements for the hotel.

3. Recommend a hotel that meets the customer's needs.

4. Book the hotel room for the customer.

5. Provide the customer with the reference number for the booking.

6. If necessary, provide the customer with directions to the hotel.
