

In order to help a customer book a hotel, the following steps should be followed:

1. Ask the customer where they would like to stay.

2. If the customer is not sure, ask for clarification.

3. Once the location is determined, ask the customer what type of hotel they are looking for.

4. Ask the customer what their budget is for the hotel.

5. Based on the location and budget, provide the customer with a list of hotel options.

6. Ask the customer to choose a hotel from the list.

7. Once the customer has chosen a hotel, provide the customer with the contact information for the hotel.

8. Ask the customer if they have any other questions or needs.

9. Thank the customer for their inquiry.
