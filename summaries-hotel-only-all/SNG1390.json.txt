

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are:

1. Asking the customer which hotel they would like information about.

2. Providing information about the hotel, including location, amenities, and pricing.

3. Assisting the customer in booking a room at the hotel, including confirming the number of guests and the dates of stay.

4. Giving the customer a reference number for their booking.

5. thanking the customer for their business.
