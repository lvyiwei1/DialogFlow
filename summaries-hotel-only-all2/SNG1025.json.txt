

1. The customer must specify their location preference (in this case, the north side).

2. The customer must specify their price range preference.

3. Based on the customer's location and price preferences, the service provider identifies the Acorn Guest House as a possible option.

4. The customer must specify their desired dates of stay.

5. Based on the customer's desired dates of stay, the service provider checks the availability of the Acorn Guest House.

6. If the Acorn Guest House is not available for the customer's desired dates of stay, the service provider identifies another possible option that fits the customer's criteria.

7. If the Acorn Guest House is available for the customer's desired dates of stay, the service provider books the room and provides the customer with a reference number.
