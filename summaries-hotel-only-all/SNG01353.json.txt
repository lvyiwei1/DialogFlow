

1. Ask what kind of accommodation the customer is looking for.
2. Find out what price range the customer is looking for.
3. Ask what part of town the customer is interested in staying in.
4. Ask what dates the customer needs the accommodation for.
5. Find out how many people will be staying in the accommodation.
6. Try to book the accommodation that the customer wants.
7. If the accommodation is not available, try to find similar accommodation that is available.
8. If the customer is still not able to find available accommodation, try to find accommodation for a different time frame.
9. Once the accommodation is booked, give the customer the reference number.
