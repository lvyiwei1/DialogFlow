

1. Ask the customer which type of accommodation they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer what their budget is for accommodation.

3. Ask the customer if they have any preferences for location.

4. Once the customer has provided all relevant information, search for accommodation options that meet their needs.

5. Present the customer with a selection of options and allow them to choose which one they would like to book.

6. Once the customer has chosen an option, provide the necessary information to complete the booking (e.g. credit card details).
