2. If the customer is looking for a specific type of hotel that is not available, ask if they would be willing to consider other types of hotels.

3. Once the customer has selected a type of hotel, ask if they have any specific requirements.

4. Search for hotels that meet the customer's requirements.

5. Ask the customer if they would like to book one of the hotels that was found.

6. If the customer would like to book a hotel, provide the customer with the reference number for the booking.
