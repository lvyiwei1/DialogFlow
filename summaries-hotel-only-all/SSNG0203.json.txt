 (in order)

1. Ask the customer whether they are looking for a hotel or a guesthouse.
2. If they are looking for a guesthouse, ask them if they have a preference for an area.
3. Find a guesthouse in the moderate price range that is available for the dates and number of people the customer needs.
4. Ask the customer if they would like to book that guesthouse.
5. If the customer wants to book the guesthouse, help them to do so and give them the booking number.
