

In order to help a customer book a hotel, the key steps are as follows:

1. Ask the customer what their budget is for the hotel.

2. Ask the customer what their preferred location is for the hotel.

3. Based on the budget and location, recommend a few different hotels to the customer.

4. Ask the customer if they would like to book any of the recommended hotels.

5. If the customer chooses to book a hotel, help them to complete the booking process.
