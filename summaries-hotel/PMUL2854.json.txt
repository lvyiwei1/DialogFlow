

In order to book a hotel for a customer, the following steps must be taken: 
1. Get the customer's desired location for the hotel. 
2. Ask if the customer has a preferred type of hotel. 
3. Get the customer's budget for the hotel stay. 
4. Find hotels in the area that meet the customer's criteria. 
5. Contact the hotel to book the reservation. 
6. Get the confirmation number from the hotel. 
7. Give the customer the confirmation number and other important information about the hotel reservation.
