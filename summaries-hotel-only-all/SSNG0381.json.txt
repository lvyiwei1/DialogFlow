3. If the reservation is unsuccessful, try to find a similar hotel in the same price range.
4. If there are no similar hotels, ask the customer if they are willing to consider a different area.
5. Try to find a hotel in the different area.
6. If a hotel is found, give the customer the details and ask if they want to proceed with the booking.
7. If the customer confirms, book the hotel and give them the reference number.
