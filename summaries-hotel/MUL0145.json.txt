 

Assuming you are starting from the beginning: 
1. Ask the customer what they are looking for in a hotel 
2. Ask the customer their budget 
3. Find hotels that match the customer criteria 
4. Ask the customer if they would like to book one of the hotels 
5. If the customer schedules a time for the reservation, book the hotel  
6. Ask the customer if there is anything else they need 
7. Thank the customer
