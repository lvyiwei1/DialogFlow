



The key steps to help a customer book a hotel according to the dialog are as follows:

1. Ask the customer what price range they are looking for.
2. Identify a 4-star hotel in the north area that fits the price range.
3. Ask the customer if they would like to book.
4. If the customer would like to book, ask for the number of nights and guests.
5. Book the room and give the customer the reference number.
6. Ask if the customer needs any further assistance.
