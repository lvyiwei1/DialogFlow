

1. Ask the customer what type of hotel they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer if they have a specific hotel in mind.

3. If the customer does not have a specific hotel in mind, suggest a few options.

4. Ask the customer if they would like to book a room at the suggested hotel.

5. If the customer would like to book a room, collect the necessary information (e.g. number of guests, dates of stay, etc.).

6. Book the room and provide the customer with a reference number.

7. Ask the customer if they need help with anything else.
