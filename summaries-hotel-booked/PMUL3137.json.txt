

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are:

1. Ask the customer when they would like to arrive and depart.

2. Ask the customer how many people will be staying.

3. Ask the customer if they have any specific requests or needs.

4. Search for hotels that match the customer's criteria.

5. Ask the customer if they would like to book a room.

6. If the customer would like to book a room, confirm the details and provide the customer with a reference number.
