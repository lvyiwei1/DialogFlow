

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel are as follows:

1. Ask the customer what kind of hotel they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer what their budget is.

3. Ask the customer what part of town they want to stay in.

4. Ask the customer what their preferred check-in date is.

5. Ask the customer how many people will be staying in the room.

6. Book the hotel room for the customer.

7. Provide the customer with the contact information for the hotel.

8. Ask the customer if they need any other assistance.
