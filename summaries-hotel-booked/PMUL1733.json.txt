

In order to help a customer book a hotel, the following steps need to be taken:

1. Ask the customer which day they would like to travel.

2. Find a train that leaves on the day the customer wants to travel.

3. Book the train for the customer.

4. Ask the customer how many tickets they need.

5. Find a hotel that meets the customer's needs in terms of star rating and price range.

6. Book the hotel for the customer.
