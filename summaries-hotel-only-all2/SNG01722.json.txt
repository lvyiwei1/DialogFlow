

1) The customer should have the name, address and phone number of the hotel they wish to book.
2) The customer should call the hotel and ask for available dates for their stay.
3) The customer should provide the hotel with the number of people staying and the number of days they will be staying.
4) The customer should give the hotel their credit card information to hold the reservation.
5) The hotel will provide the customer with a reference number for their booking.
