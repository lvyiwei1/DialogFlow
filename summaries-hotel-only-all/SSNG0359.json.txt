

1. Ask what the customer's budget is for a hotel.
2. Ask what area of town the customer wants to stay in.
3. Ask what star rating the customer wants for their hotel.
4. Ask how many people will be staying in the room and for how many nights.
5. Ask if the customer has any specific requirements for their stay (internet, parking, etc).
6. Try to book a hotel that meets the customer's requirements.
7. If the first hotel booking is unsuccessful, try to book a different hotel in the same price range.
8. Confirm with the customer that the booking is successful and thank them for their business.
