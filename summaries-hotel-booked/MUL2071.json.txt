

1. Ask the customer if they know what kind of lodging they want (e.g. hotel, guesthouse).
2. If the customer wants a hotel, ask them if they have a preferred location.
3. If the customer does not have a preferred location, suggest areas.
4. If the customer wants a guesthouse, tell them how many are available.
5. Ask the customer if they have a price range in mind.
6. If the customer does not have a price range in mind, suggest price ranges.
7. Book the hotel for the customer.
8. Provide the customer with a reference number.
