

1. The customer contacts the TownInfo center and asks for a train to Ely.
2. The TownInfo agent asks the customer for their desired arrival time in Ely.
3. The TownInfo agent provides the customer with several train options, including departure times and arrival times.
4. The customer chooses a train and requests a reservation.
5. The TownInfo agent provides the customer with the reference number for their train.
6. The TownInfo agent asks the customer if they need help finding a hotel.
7. The TownInfo agent provides the customer with information about several nearby hotels, including amenities and rates.
8. The customer chooses a hotel and requests a reservation.
9. The TownInfo agent provides the customer with the address and contact information for their hotel.
