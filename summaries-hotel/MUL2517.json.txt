2. Ask the customer if they have a preference for location.
3. Ask the customer if they have any specific requirements for their accommodation.
4. Suggest some accommodation options that meet the customer's needs.
5. Ask the customer if they would like to book a room at one of the suggested options.
6. If the customer would like to book a room, ask for details such as dates of stay and number of guests.
7. Book the room for the customer and provide confirmation details.
8. Thank the customer and let them know to contact again if they need anything else.
