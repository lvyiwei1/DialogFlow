

1. Ask the customer what they are looking for in a hotel.

2. If the customer is looking for a specific type of hotel, see if the business has that type of hotel.

3. If the business does not have the specific type of hotel the customer is looking for, suggest a similar hotel.

4. Ask the customer if they would like to book the hotel.

5. If the customer wants to book the hotel, help them with the process.

6. If the customer does not want to book the hotel, thank them for their time.
