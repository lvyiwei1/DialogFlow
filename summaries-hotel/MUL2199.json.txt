

The key steps for helping a customer book a hotel would be as follows:

1. Ask the customer what day they are looking to travel.

2. Find a train that depart from Cambridge and arrives in Norwich at a price that the customer is willing to pay.

3. Ask the customer if they need a place to stay.

4. Find a three star guesthouse in the area that the customer is interested in.

5. Book the hotel for the customer.
