

1. Ask the customer what kind of hotel they are looking for.

2. Ask the customer how many nights they will be staying.

3. Ask the customer how many people will be staying in the room.

4. Ask the customer if they have any specific requirements for the hotel, such as proximity to a mall or restaurants.

5. If the customer is booking for multiple people, ask if they have any specific requirements for each person.

6. Once the customer has provided all of the necessary information, search for hotels that match their criteria.

7. If there are multiple hotels that match the customer's criteria, provide the customer with a choice of hotels.

8. Once the customer has selected a hotel, confirm the details of the booking and provide the customer with a reference number.
